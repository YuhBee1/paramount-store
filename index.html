Common Questions

How do I track my order?
Visit the Track Order page and enter your tracking ID or order email.
What is your return policy?
Returns of unused products in original packaging within 7 days of delivery. Contact us with your order reference.
How long does delivery take?
Uyo & Akwa Ibom: same/next day. South-South: 1–2 days. Nationwide: 3–5 days. International: on request.
Payment failed but I was charged?
Failed payments auto-reverse in 3–5 business days. Submit a ticket with your bank reference.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be changed/cancelled within 2 hours. After dispatch, call or WhatsApp us immediately.
